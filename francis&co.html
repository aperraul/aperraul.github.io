Francis & Co - RPG 2D combat / 3D exploration

School project.

RPG with 3D exploration mode and 2D combat mode, made with Unreal Engine (Blueprint) with 4 programmers, 6 game designers, 8 game artists and 1 sound designer.

Get along with Ben, Jess and Lisa in their rusty but trusty van! Follow the summer vacation these teenagers in their quest of being the best skate gang of San Francisco!

Drive in the beautiful city of San Francisco. Discover the most iconic neighborhoods and places in this city full of history! Interact with the locals, get to know them and their story. Help them by doing quests, they'll sure know how to reward you!

Master a variety of skateboarding tricks and use them in a fast-paced, turn-based races against your opponents!
