Break The Chain - 3D Action Narration

School project.

2D platformer made in 10 weeks with Unity, my team was composed of 4 game programmer, 6 game artists, and 2 music and sound designers. The main challenge of this project was to learn teamwork with other trades.

As game programmers, we had to implement 2 gameplay features from an imposed list of 8 on 2 different levels.

Azaan is a rapid pace platformer where the player has to reach checkpoints before his life wore off, in this game, we combined the imposed features "dashes" and "ziplines" with the smooth movements of our character to create bring these constant feelings of speed and lightness.
