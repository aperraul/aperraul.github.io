Break The Chain - 3D Action Narration

School project.

3D Narrative game made in 10 weeks with Unreal Engine (Blueprint) with 4 gameplay programmers.

In this game you are an employee of a tyrannical production chain factory which puts you in competition with the other workers. A leaderboard shows the employee ranking at all time and something in your mind tells you that you'd better not end up at the bottom of the ranks.

Are you going to work hard to be the best worker or are you going to find a way to escape this place?
